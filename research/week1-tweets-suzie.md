## 🎯 Suzie's Notes for Matt

A few things to keep in mind as you post this week:

**Reply to everyone.** In Week 1, you have no followers yet — so every reply you get is gold. Respond personally, not with a canned line. "Thanks!" is a waste of a reply. "Oh man, the Tuesday 4pm scramble is the worst — which sport?" is how you build a follower for life.

**Don't link-dump.** Notice none of these tweets send people to KinSyncApp.com (except the Day 4 CTA). That's intentional. Trust first, sell second. Links suppress reach anyway.

**The Wednesday question is your most important tweet.** Read every reply carefully. These people are describing your product roadmap out loud. Screenshot the best ones — they'll become future tweets.

**Saturday's tweet will be your most liked.** Warm personal content about family almost always outperforms product content. Let it breathe. Don't add a CTA.

**You've got this.** Seven tweets, one week, one real story. That's all it takes to start.
